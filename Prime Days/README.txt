Takes in a number of ints representing the length of months in a made up year. For instance 5 5 10 would be a year of 20 days with the numbers being the break up of the months. The task is to return the true prime days, true prime being prime day of year, prime month and prime day of month. For 5 5 10 it would return,
7: 2 2
13: 3 3
17: 3 7
Day of year: Month of year, day of month
